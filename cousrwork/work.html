As soon as you have completely filled out the order form, you will automatically receive personal membership area at TopDissertations.com.

When you want to check the status of your order, upload or download anything, all you have to do is to log on to your account using your email address and password. The status of your order changes several times. If the status says it’s “paid”, it means that you will soon be assigned a writer to work on your order, it’s “in progress”, it means that there is already a writer assigned to work on your order and that you may now be able to contact him or her. If the status says it’s “completed”, it means that your custom written paper is now ready for download.

Our payment process is quite simple. We accept all major debit and credit cards. All payments are made through secure systems, which give you total confidentiality and security of financial operations. We give you our guarantees, so you don't have to worry about any personal information provided to us or about payment insecure.

Your order then begins to be processed. When it’s completed, your dissertation will be subjected to a plagiarism check to make sure that it’s original. After ensuring that it is, your order status will change to “completed”, which means that it is now ready for download.
